# Assumptions

## Data Access

- No internal analytics accounts were accessed (Google Analytics, Meta Business Suite, Google Ads, etc.).
- No backend systems, CRMs, or internal dashboards were consulted.

## Data Sources

All findings are based on publicly available sources:

- **Meta Ad Library** -- active and historical ad creatives
- **Google Ads Transparency Center** -- advertiser activity and ad history
- **PageSpeed Insights** -- web performance metrics (Core Web Vitals)

## Accuracy

- Spend estimates are **directional only** and inferred from public signals. They do not reflect actual account-level data.
- Strategy recommendations are directional and require validation with real account data before implementation.

## Data Capture Date

All performance data and ad library snapshots were captured on **17 March 2026**.
